**Any commercial use requires a paid commercial license.** That includes,

- offering services built on Portcullis (managed audits, consulting

- redistributing it as part of a commercial product.

## How to get a commercial license

Open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/yakohhhh/portcullis/issues/new)
titled `Commercial license inquiry` (you do not need to disclose anything
sensitive publicly - just leave a way to reach you), or contact the
maintainer directly through the contact details on the
[@yakohhhh](https://github.com/yakohhhh) GitHub profile.

Please include:

- your organization's name and size;
- the intended use (internal audits, client services, product integration);
- the number of users or scanned environments, roughly.

Terms are simple and priced to be accessible; small structures get small
prices.
